We're excited to introduce the Integrated Plugin Manager, a powerful new feature that seamlessly connects your SaaS application with external tools and services. Automate workflows, enhance communication, and streamline operations by integrating with popular business platforms like Zapier, Slack, and more.

You can now extend your workspace effortlessly:

- **New**: Centralized integrations marketplace included by default.
- **One-click setup**: Browse categories and install integrations directly from the Apps section.
- **Secure connections**: Authenticate with external services and configure permissions safely.
- **Works with**: Automation tools, email marketing platforms, notification services, and more.
- **Extensible**: Add custom plugins to fit your unique business needs.

Ready to automate your workflows? Head to Apps > Integrations and start connecting today.
