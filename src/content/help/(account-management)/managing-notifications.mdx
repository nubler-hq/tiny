## Introduction

The Notifications page lets you customize how you receive communications from SaaS Boilerplate. You can control transactional notifications (essential account updates) and marketing notifications (newsletters and platform updates). By the end of this guide, you'll have your notification preferences set to match your needs.

<Callout title="Tip">Customize notifications to receive only what matters to you, reducing inbox clutter while staying informed.</Callout>

## Prerequisites

- Access to the SaaS Boilerplate dashboard
- A valid account with notification settings permissions

## Core Concepts

### Transactional Notifications
Essential communications about your account, security, usage limits, and system messages. These are critical and should generally remain enabled.

### Marketing Notifications
Optional communications including newsletters, tips, platform updates, and promotional content. You can opt in or out based on your preferences.

## Accessing Notification Settings

Navigate to Settings > Account > Notifications in the dashboard sidebar to view and modify your preferences.

## Notification Categories

<Accordions type="multiple">
<Accordion title="Transactional Notifications">
These are essential for account management:
- Account updates and security alerts
- Usage limit notifications
- System messages and critical updates
- Sales notifications (can be toggled on/off)
- Periodic reports (can be toggled on/off)
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Marketing Notifications">
Optional communications to keep you informed:
- Newsletters with tips and best practices
- Platform updates and new features
- Promotional content and announcements
</Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Delivery Methods

Notifications can be delivered through multiple channels:

- **Email**: Primary method for most notifications
- **In-App**: Real-time notifications within the dashboard
- **Push**: Browser notifications (if enabled)

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="multiple">
<Accordion title="Not receiving notifications">
Check your email spam folder and ensure the correct email address is set in your profile. Verify notification preferences are enabled.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Too many notifications">
Review and disable non-essential marketing notifications. Focus on keeping transactional notifications active.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Settings not updating">
Refresh the page and try again. If issues persist, contact support.
</Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Best Practices

<Accordions type="multiple">
