## Introduction

Customizing your organization helps create a professional and branded experience for your team and clients. This guide covers setting up your logo, organization details, and social media connections. By the end, your workspace will reflect your brand identity.

<Callout title="Tip">Consistent branding builds team pride and establishes credibility with clients.</Callout>

## Prerequisites

- Owner or Admin access to organization settings
- Logo file in PNG, SVG, or high-quality JPEG format
- Social media profiles (optional but recommended)

## Core Concepts

### Organization Profile
Your organization's basic information including name, logo, and contact details.

### Branding Elements
Visual and textual elements that represent your organization consistently.

### Social Integration
Connecting your social media presence to your workspace for better engagement.

## Step-by-Step Guide

<Steps>
<Step>
### Access Organization Settings
Navigate to Settings > Organization > Information in the dashboard.
</Step>

<Step>
### Upload Logo
Click the upload button and select your logo file. Ensure it's high-resolution for crisp display.
</Step>

<Step>
### Set Organization Details
Enter your organization name, slug, contact email, and website URL.
</Step>

<Step>
### Add Social Media Links
Connect your LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, and other social profiles.
</Step>

<Step>
### Save Changes
Click Save to apply your customizations across the platform.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="multiple">
<Accordion title="Logo appears blurry">
Upload a higher resolution image (minimum 300x300 pixels) in PNG or SVG format.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Social links not updating">
Refresh the page and ensure URLs are correct. Changes may take a moment to propagate.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Organization name conflicts">
Choose a unique slug. If taken, try variations with numbers or industry terms.
</Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Best Practices

<Accordions type="multiple">
<Accordion title="Use consistent branding">
Apply the same colors, fonts, and style across all platforms for brand recognition.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Keep info current">
Regularly update contact information and social links as your organization evolves.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Involve your team">
Get input from team members on branding decisions to build buy-in.
</Accordion>
</Accordions>
