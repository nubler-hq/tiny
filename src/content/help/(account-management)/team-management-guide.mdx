## Introduction

Effective team management is crucial for scaling your organization in SaaS Boilerplate. This guide covers inviting members, assigning roles, and maintaining a productive collaborative environment. By the end, you'll have a well-structured team with clear permissions and responsibilities.

<Callout title="Tip">Clear roles and communication help teams collaborate more effectively and securely.</Callout>

## Prerequisites

- Owner or Admin access to the organization
- Understanding of your team's needs and responsibilities
- Access to the dashboard settings

## Core Concepts

### Team Roles
SaaS Boilerplate uses a hierarchical role system: Owner, Admin, and Member, each with specific permissions.

### Permissions
Roles determine what actions users can perform, from basic access to full administrative control.

### Invitations
New members receive email invitations with setup instructions and role assignments.

## Step-by-Step Guide

<Steps>
<Step>
### Access Team Management
Navigate to Settings > Organization > Members in the dashboard.
</Step>

<Step>
### Invite a New Member
Click "Invite Member" and enter:
- Email address
- Role (Owner, Admin, or Member)
- Optional welcome message
</Step>

<Step>
### Send Invitation
Click "Send Invitation". The member will receive an email with login instructions.
</Step>

<Step>
### Manage Existing Members
View the member list, change roles via dropdown, or remove members as needed.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="multiple">
<Accordion title="Invitation not received">
Check spam folder or resend invitation. Verify email address is correct.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Member can't access features">
Review their role permissions. Promote if needed or adjust settings.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can't remove Owner">
Contact support to transfer ownership before removal.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Role changes not applying">
Refresh the page or check for confirmation. Changes are usually immediate.
</Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Best Practices

<Accordions type="multiple">
<Accordion title="Assign roles carefully">
Start with Member role and promote based on responsibilities and trust.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Communicate expectations">
Explain roles and permissions during onboarding to avoid confusion.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Regular audits">
Review team access quarterly and adjust as team structure evolves.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Remove access promptly">
Revoke permissions immediately when team members leave.
</Accordion>
</Accordions>
