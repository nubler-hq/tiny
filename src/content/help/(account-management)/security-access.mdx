## Introduction

The Security & Access page is your control center for account protection. Here, you can enable two-factor authentication, connect external accounts for easier login, and manage active sessions. By the end of this guide, your account will be more secure and easier to manage.

<Callout title="Tip">Regular security reviews help protect your account and data.</Callout>

## Prerequisites

- Access to the SaaS Boilerplate dashboard
- A compatible device for 2FA setup (smartphone with authenticator app)
- External accounts (Google, GitHub, etc.) if you want to connect them

## Core Concepts

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
Adds an extra security layer by requiring a code from your phone in addition to your password.

### Connected Accounts
Link external services like Google or GitHub for single sign-on, making login faster and more secure.

### Session Management
View and control all devices and browsers currently logged into your account.

## Step-by-Step Guide

<Steps>
<Step>
### Access Security Settings
Navigate to Settings > Account > Security & Access in the dashboard.
</Step>

<Step>
### Connect External Accounts
Click "Connect" next to supported providers (Google, GitHub, Microsoft) and follow the authorization prompts.
</Step>

<Step>
### Enable Two-Factor Authentication
Click "Enable 2FA", choose your method (authenticator app or SMS), scan the QR code, and enter the verification code.
</Step>

<Step>
### Manage Sessions
Review the list of active sessions, and click "End Session" for any you don't recognize or want to terminate.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="multiple">
<Accordion title="2FA setup fails">
Ensure your authenticator app is up to date and you're entering the code correctly. Try SMS as an alternative.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can't connect external account">
Verify your external account credentials and ensure the provider is supported. Check for any account restrictions.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Session won't end">
Refresh the page and try again. If persistent, contact support.
</Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Best Practices

<Accordions type="multiple">

</Accordion>
