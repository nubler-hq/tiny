## Workspace and Team Management

<Accordions type="multiple">
<Accordion title="How do I customize my workspace?">
Go to Settings > Organization > Information to upload your logo, set your organization name, connect social profiles, and personalize your workspace to match your brand.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="How do I invite team members?">
Use the team management section to invite people by email. They'll receive a professional invitation and can join immediately with appropriate roles (Owner, Admin, or Member).
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="How do I manage access permissions?">
Assign roles to control what each team member can do: Owners have full control, Admins can manage team and settings, Members can use tools but not change configurations.
</Accordion>
</Accordions>
