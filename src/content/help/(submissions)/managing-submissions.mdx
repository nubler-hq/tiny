## Introduction

The Submissions page is your central hub for tracking and managing form submissions from your business forms and contact pages. Access it via the Submissions section in the dashboard sidebar. This system provides tools for sharing forms, tracking submissions, and exporting data for analysis. By the end of this guide, you'll know how to handle submissions effectively.

<Callout title="Tip">Regular submission management helps improve user engagement and data quality.</Callout>

## Prerequisites

- Access to the SaaS Boilerplate dashboard
- Permission to manage submissions (Member role or higher)
- Forms set up to collect submissions

## Core Concepts

### Form Submissions
Individual responses from users who fill out your forms, containing data like name, email, and custom fields.

### Sharing Forms
Generate shareable links to distribute forms via email, social media, or websites.

### Export Functionality
Download submission data in common formats for external analysis or backup.

### Usage Limits
Monitor submission count against your plan's limits (e.g., 0/1000 used).

## Step-by-Step Guide

<Steps>
<Step>
### Access Submissions Page
Navigate to Submissions in the dashboard sidebar.
</Step>

<Step>
### Share a Form
Click "Share Form" to generate a shareable link and distribute it through your preferred channels.
</Step>

<Step>
### Monitor Submissions
View incoming submissions in the table, including name, email, form type, and submission date.
</Step>

<Step>
### Search and Filter
Use the search bar to find submissions by name or email. Sort columns as needed.
</Step>

<Step>
### Export Data
Click the export button to download submission data for analysis or backup.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="multiple">
<Accordion title="No submissions showing">
Ensure forms are published and shared. Check if submissions are being received.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Export fails">
Verify you have submissions to export and try again. Contact support if issues persist.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Share link not working">
Regenerate the link and ensure it's distributed correctly.
</Accordion>
</Accordions>
